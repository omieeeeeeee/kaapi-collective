Our Story

Started with one question

Why does India grow some of the world's finest coffee beans, yet most of it gets exported? Kaapi Collective started in 2021 as an answer to that question. We source directly from estates in Coorg, Chikmagalur, and Araku Valley, roast in small batches at our Bandra roastery, and serve it the way it deserves.

Every cup traces back to a specific farm, a specific harvest. We keep things simple: good beans, careful roasting, honest brewing. No shortcuts.
